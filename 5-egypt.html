Efforts to combat climate change
Over the past period, the Egyptian government has implemented many projects to confront climate change and achieve a green recovery

1- The projects of the Ministry of Transport for the expansion of the subway lines

2- The high-speed electric train project (Ain Sokhna - the New Administrative Capital - New Alamein, and the monorail lines of the Administrative Capital and 6th of October City)

3- New and renewable energy projects and energy efficiency (establishing wind energy power stations in the Gulf of Oman).

4- Northern coast protection projects

5- Projects dealing with rain and floods

6- A project to rehabilitate and cultivate 1.5 million feddans to achieve food security and compensate for land degradation and erosion in the Delta.

7- Projects for sustainable management of water resources to improve the efficiency of using water resources (establishing seawater desalination plants, sewage networks, tertiary treatment, rehabilitation and renewal of the national network of canals and water canals.

And about the state’s efforts to promote the transition towards a green economy and provide financing to achieve the goals of sustainable development, and what was done in this context of preparing the first national report on financing sustainable development in Egypt, and what Egypt did in terms of issuing green bonds worth $750 million, for the first time in Africa and the East. In the Middle East, with reference to what the private sector will do in launching green bonds with a value ranging between 120-200 million dollars, in addition to the efforts of the “Egypt Sovereign Fund for Investment and Development” in the field of climate change by investing in a number of green projects such as the hydrogen and ammonia production project. Green in partnership with a number of specialized international companies, and a project to produce train cars to support sustainable transportation efforts, as well as water desalination projects.

Regarding the national efforts to confront the phenomenon of climate change, the Arab Republic of Egypt is honored to host the Summit of the Conference of the Parties to the United Nations Convention on Climate Change COP 27 for the year 2022 in the city of Sharm El-Sheikh, and this conference is a reflection of the pivotal role that Egypt plays regionally and internationally. From the President of the Republic for the success of this global event, and the belief of the Ministry of Planning and Economic Development in the importance of involving young people and exploiting their energy in confronting the phenomenon of climate change, adapting to its effects, and reducing emissions.
